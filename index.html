🌌 Solar System Info

Explore our solar system with accurate planetary data from NASA/JPL. Use the controls to navigate and learn about each planet.

Available Views:

  • Solar System View - Explore planets and their orbits
  • 🌍 Earth View - Real-time NASA EONET events and natural disasters

Navigation Tips:

  • Click and drag to rotate the view
  • Scroll to zoom in/out
  • Use keyboard shortcuts for quick navigation
  • Click planet buttons to focus on specific planets

Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • Space - Pause/Resume animation
  • R - Reset camera
  • F - Toggle fullscreen
  • I - Toggle info panel
  • C - Toggle controls panel
  • 1-9 - Focus on planets (Sun to Neptune)
